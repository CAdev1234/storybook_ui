Annual Leadership Development Conference

This annual conference offers unique training, development, and networking opportunities with other leaders from across Canada, the United States and the United Kingdom. The conference is open to invited attendees, including all elected leaders and Foresters Board of Directors Scholarship winners. These scholarship recipients are chosen based on their contributions to Foresters.

Member leaders take part in workshops focusing on personal, team, family and community leadership skills, lead by industry experts and senior Foresters leadership.
