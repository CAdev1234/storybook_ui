Your branch: Greater Toronto Area
Part of being a member of Foresters is being able to get involved in governing our organization.
Branch meetings

Foresters, as a member-governed organization, focuses our efforts on the family well-being of our members and their communities. Your Branch meets regularly to discuss local member governance, review financial reports, provide oversight of local community activities, and provide input on other issues related the Branch's purpose and responsibilities. If you would like to attend a meeting of your Branch, please join us to discuss issues that can affect you, your family and your community.

Call the following number to join the meeting and, when prompted, enter your Participant Code.

Teleconference Number: 1-800-201-7439

Participant code: 425383

All meetings are about an hour and conducted via conference call, so you can attend meetings comfortably from your home.
