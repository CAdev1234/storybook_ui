Get started
Community Grants enable members to become involved in their communities by hosting or attending meaningful activities to help people in need during difficult times. As we continue to abide by local government and health authority safety guidelines, due to COVID-19, we encourage you to come together in participation of our Community Grants program. Active members who are 18 years of age and older can submit their applications now to be considered for approval. Please see eligibility details within the grants guidelines.
